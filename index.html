Press A Key to Start

Rules:

  1. Watch and Repeat: Simon will display a sequence of colored lights and sounds. Pay close attention to the order in which the colors appear.
  2. Repeat Correctly: After Simon's sequence, it's your turn to replicate the same sequence by pressing the corresponding colored buttons. Tap the buttons in the same order as Simon's sequence.
  3. Progression: If you repeat the sequence correctly, Simon will add a new color to the sequence, making it longer and more challenging. Keep track of each color as the sequence grows.
  4. Mistakes End the Game: Make a mistake by pressing the wrong color or pressing them out of order, and the game ends.
  5. Challenge Yourself: Test your memory and concentration as you strive to beat your previous high score by remembering longer and more complex sequences. Let's see how far you can go!
